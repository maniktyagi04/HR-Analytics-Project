# 🗂 Dashboard Overview

## Purpose
This dashboard analyzes factors influencing job change intent among data scientists and provides HR-focused retention insights through KPI monitoring and attrition driver analysis.

---

# 📌 KPI Summary (Top Banner)

The dashboard contains three key performance indicators:

### KPI 1 — Attrition Rate
- Overall percentage of candidates likely to switch jobs.

### KPI 2 — Average Experience
- Average years of candidate experience.

### KPI 3 — Average Training Hours
- Average training hours completed.

---

# 📈 Visualizations Included

## 1. Attrition by Experience Band
**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

Shows job change rates across:
- Fresher
- Junior
- Mid-Level
- Senior
- Veteran

**Business Question:**
Which experience segment shows highest attrition risk?

---

## 2. Education Level Distribution of Attrition
**Chart Type:** Stacked Bar Chart

Shows:
- Switching vs Non-Switching candidates
- Split by education level

**Business Question:**
Does education influence job mobility?

---

## 3. City Tier vs Attrition
**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

Compares job change rates across city tiers.

**Business Question:**
Does urban development impact switching behavior?

---

## 4. Education × Company Size Attrition Heatmap
**Chart Type:** Heatmap

Shows high-risk combinations using:
- Education Level
- Company Size

**Business Question:**
Which employee segments have highest switching risk?

---

# 🎛 Interactive Filters

Dashboard includes filters for:

- Gender  
- Education Level  
- Company Size  

These filters apply across all visualizations.
